GFA Regional Symposium

October 29th, 2017

GFA event information

Guitar Foundation of America Regional Symposium is coming to Texas for the first time, hosted by Dr. Fernand Vera and Dr. Olga Amelkina-Vera at Collin College Spring Creek Campus in Plano, TX on October 29th, 2017!

Our guest conductor this year is the world-famous Bill Kanengiser, so don't miss it! Workshops will be taught by Bill Kanengiser and Collin College favorite guest faculty, Mitch Weverka.

For more information, checkout the GFA event information for registration and schedule. We can't wait to see so many friends from Texas and beyond at this exciting one-day event! Share widely and see you soon!!
