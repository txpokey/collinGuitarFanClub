11th Annual Collin College Guitar Competition

November 4, 2017

Dr. Fernand Vera

The Eleventh Annual Guitar Competition kicks off at 12 p.m. on Saturday, Nov. 4 at the Spring Creek Campus, located at 2800 E. Spring Creek Parkway in Plano.

The competition is open to all students currently enrolled at a high school or Texas community college. Students can compete in a High School Division (seven minute program), Community College Freshman Division (eight minute program), or Community College Sophomore Division (12 minute program).

The deadline to apply is Nov. 1st and the application fee is $40. The event is free for Collin College students, but a donation of $25 is suggested. application can be downloaded HERE

For more information, contact Dr. Fernand Vera at fvera@collin.edu or 972.881.5613.
