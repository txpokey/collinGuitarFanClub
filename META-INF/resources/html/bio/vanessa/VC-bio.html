Vanessa Corder

Associate Faculty

Classical Guitar

facebook

Vanessa Corder was born in Tampico, Mexico to an Italian father and British mother. They relocated to Texas when she was two and she began playing the classical guitar at the age of six. She then continued to study privately for the next ten years.

Beginning in 2001 Vanessa attended Collin College, where she pursued professional guitar training on scholarship and studied under Sabine Madriguera. She later transferred to Southern Methodist University in 2004, where she completed both her Bachelor and Master of Music Degrees in Guitar Performance on scholarship, studying under Robert Guthrie, and graduated with honors. Vanessa has performed in several master classes and has won prizes competing in guitar competitions.

Vanessa has enjoyed teaching in her private studio since 2002. She also enjoys performing for various events around the DFW metroplex.
