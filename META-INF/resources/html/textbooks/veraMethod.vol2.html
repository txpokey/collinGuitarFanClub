The Beginnging Guitar Method

This book is a continuation of the skills learned in Vol. 1. Some of the new concepts are reading music in upper positions, more complex rhythmic examples, playing two-voice music, and new techniques such as left hand slurs, scales, and arpeggios. The book also includes several solo pieces, ensemble works, and sight-reading examples in various positions.

This textbook currently is only available through the college's bookstore.
